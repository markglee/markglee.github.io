How to sit an Exam

Dr. Mark Lee

23rd May 2018

Introduction
In previous years during my pre-exam revision lectures, I've always said that most current students should be the real exam experts. I haven't sat an exam for 20 odd years and most undergraduates will have completed many exams since the age of 16. I think this is still correct however each year I complete my exam marking, I feel less sure. Every year I mark scripts where it's obvious that the student doesn't get it regardless of whether or not they understand any of the computer science I've tried my best to teach.

Despite this, I suspect if we ran a "how to take an exam" lecture, few would attend and most would regard it a waste of time. Hence this page.
