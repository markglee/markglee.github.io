I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Computer Science. My research interests are focussed on Natural Language Processing. I'm interested in Sentiment Analysis of text, the automatic identification and understanding of metaphor and the effects of pragmatic inference in dialogue processing. More recently I've been investigating the extraction of constraints from text to build formal models for reasoning.
For 2015-2016 I'm teaching the following modules:
LC Language and Logic (Canvas)
LI Professional Computing (Canvas)
I am Deputy Head of School and Head of Student Development and Support for Computer Science. I also chair the College of Engineering and Physical Science's Student Experience Group.
